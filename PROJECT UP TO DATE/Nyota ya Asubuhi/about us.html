About Us

Welcome to our Children's Home.

We are dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing environment for children in need.

We have a team of dedicated staff and volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure the well-being of every child in our care. Our programs include education, healthcare, and recreational activities designed to help children thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support their growth and development through various programs and activities.

Together with the volunteers and donors, is to become a family for children bereft of parental care.

We create and support Agape love, stability, and inclusion to help each member of the Nyota ya Asubuhi family realize their divine worth and potential. We do this by providing for the social, physical, intellectualz and spiritual needs of those we serve.

We believe that every child deserves a loving home and a bright future. Thank you for visiting our website and learning more about our work.
